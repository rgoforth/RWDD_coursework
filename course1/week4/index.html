Scoring Leaders By Position (ESPN)

Aaron Rodgers QB

Green Bay Packers

Despite real time struggles, Rodgers has been on fire in fantasy terms this year. He looks primed to continue the roll Monday night in Philly.

David Johnson RB

Arizona Cardinals

The most consistent Cardinal player leads in receiving yards by a back and currently sits tied atop the total touchdown by RBs. If you have this dual threat nightmare for opposing defenses keep rolling him out.

Mike Evans WR

Tampa Bay Buccaneers

Evans has produced at least 70 yards and a score in all but 4 games. Even with a tough matchup against the LoB, you've almost got to play him due to his size advantage and high target share.

Greg Olsen TE

Carolina Panthers

Despite a few rough weeks as of late, Olsen continues to be the leader at a position that hasn't been great for season leagues consistently. Anyways, the 7th Floor Crew alum gets a favorable matchup against the Raiders this Sunday.

Minnesota Vikings D/ST

Aside from 4 games this unit has been phenomenal. Certainly having Cordarrelle Patterson as a returner proves very nice as seen last week.

Matt Bryant K

Atlanta Falcons

23/25 FG, those two misses came from 50+ distance. He does have one missed XP on the year, which we saw in Week 11, is bound to happen to happen around the league.
